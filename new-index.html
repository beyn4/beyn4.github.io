Nadia Bey

If you asked me about my dream career as a kid, I probably would've said "doctor or author." Years later, I think my younger self would be proud to see the writer and researcher she'd become.

My interest in healthcare started at a young age, but as I gained more exposure to public health and healthcare systems, I found myself questioning how good health could become attainable to all.

This interest coexisted with my love for writing. Writing is more than just a stress reliever — it's a way to explore ideas and explain complex topics to others. This has influenced my interest in journalism, especially health reporting and investigations. I believe everyone should have equal access to information, and I work in service of this goal.

I'm a lot of other things, too. I'm also a coder and pop culture lover that loves creating things.

This website is a snapshot of what I've done. My academic background can be found here, published writing can be found here, and projects I've worked on can be found here.
