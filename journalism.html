I started writing for publications in my sophomore year of high school, contributing to Issues 6 through 10 of the quarterly student-led zine The Academic. I then went on to write entertainment and current event articles for Affinity Magazine, where I currently act as Books Editor. At Duke, I am the managing editor of the independent student newspaper, The Chronicle, and I was previously co-president of Duke's chapter of Her Campus. I have also written for the Journal for Young Investigators and other publications.
